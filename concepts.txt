Die: has 6 Faces
Face: has an Ability, #pips, and Mods.
Ability: has name, sprite, type, list of Patterns, +more
Pattern: has bitstring defining layout, +more
Mod: has name, sprite, +more

Monster: has sprite, list of Faces, Ability Timings and Movement Timings
Ability/Movement Timing: list of booleans representing if the abil or movement happens at that index of time
